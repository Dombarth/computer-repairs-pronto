About Us

At Computer Repairs Pronto we have been providing computer repair services across the Hills District for over nineteen years. The business started in 2006 out of a small workshop near Castle Hill and we are still based there today.

Our Team

The team pride themselves on providing quality repairs while still remaining competitive on price. We handle all types of computer repairs from laptops and desktops through to Macs and business IT systems. Our technicians are trained on all major brands and we have seen pretty much every problem there is over the years.

We dont use subcontractors on any job. Its always one of our own technicians who will work on your machine. As such you can be sure the work is done to our standards.

What We Do

We handle all the common stuff like virus removal, slow computers, software problems and hardware failures. Also the more complex repairs like liquid damage, screen replacements, data recovery from failed drives and motherboard issues. The team are equiped to handle most computer problems that come through the door.

Our Workshop

We operate out of a workshop near Castle Hill. Customers can drop machines off during business hours. For straightforward repairs we can often offer same day turnaround depending on parts availablity.

We provide a free assesment and quote before starting any work so there are no surprises.

Insurance and Warranty

We are fully insured with public liability coverage. All repairs come with a warranty on parts and labour. The business is Australian owned and has been operating in the Hills District since 2006.
