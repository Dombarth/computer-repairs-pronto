Game Console Repair in Sydney, NSW

At Computer Repairs Pronto we specialise in the areas of gaming console repairs for customers in Sydney and the surrounding western Sydney areas. The team have been fixing Xbox, PlayStation and Nintendo Switch consoles for over twelve years now and we are fully equipped to handle pretty much anything that comes through the door. We offer free quotes over the phone or in store and most console repairs are completed same day.

We do not send your console off to anyone else. The technicians at Computer Repairs Pronto carry out all repairs on site at our workshop which is how we keep turnaround times as fast as they are. We are fully insured and hold public liability and all console repair work carried out comes with a warranty on parts and labour. As such we are able to offer a level of service that alot of the other console repair shops in the area simply do not.

What We Fix

  • HDMI port replacement
  • Cooling fan replacement
  • Power supply faults
  • SSD and storage upgrades
  • Disc drive repairs
  • Joy-Con drift repairs
  • Charging port replacements
  • General cleaning and maintainence

Thats the main stuff but if its something outside of that list give us a call and the team can let you know if its something we can help with.

PlayStation Repair

We carry out PlayStation repairs on all models including PS4, PS4 Pro, PS5 and PS5 Digital Edition. The most common PlayStation repair we see is HDMI port replacement where the port has been damaged or become loose. This is a board level repair that requires micro soldering and its not something every repair shop can do. We also handle power supply faults, overheating issues, disc drive failures and general board level diagnostics on PlayStation consoles.

Xbox Repair

For Xbox consoles we repair Xbox One, Xbox One S, Xbox One X, Xbox Series S and Xbox Series X. Similar to PlayStation the most common fault is HDMI port damage. We also see alot of overheating issues on older Xbox One models and power supply faults across the range. The technicians at Computer Repairs Pronto carry out all Xbox repairs at the board level where required which means we can fix faults that other shops would charge a full board replacement for.

Nintendo Switch Repair

Nintendo Switch repairs are another common job for us. Joy-Con drift is probably the single most common Switch fault we see followed by charging port issues on the Switch and Switch Lite. We also repair screen faults, rail connector issues and board level faults on all Switch models including the OLED version. For handheld console repairs more broadly we work on Switch, Switch Lite, Steam Deck and older handheld systems.

Console Repairs Across Sydney

We are based in western Sydney and the majority of our console repair customers come from across the local area. We service customers from Parramatta, Castle Hill, Baulkham Hills, Northmead, the hills district and surrounding suburbs. We also get people driving in from further across western Sydney and up from the north shore which is no problem at all.
