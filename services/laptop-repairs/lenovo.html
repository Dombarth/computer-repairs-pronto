Lenovo ThinkPad Repairs

The ThinkPad is legendary in the business laptop world. Originally an IBM product before Lenovo acquired the brand in 2005. ThinkPads are known for their reliability and excellent keyboards. We see alot of ThinkPads from corporate customers and small businesses across the Hills District.

Common Lenovo ThinkPad Models We Repair

  • ThinkPad X1 Carbon (Gen 9, 10, 11, 12) - Premium ultrabook. Screen issues, keyboard problems, fan noise.
  • ThinkPad X1 Yoga (Gen 6, 7, 8, 9) - Convertible model. Hinge wear, touchscreen issues, pen problems.
  • ThinkPad T Series (T14, T14s, T15, T16) - Mainstream business. Keyboards, screens, batteries.
  • ThinkPad L Series (L14, L15, L16) - Budget business. Similar issues to T Series but more common.
  • ThinkPad E Series (E14, E15, E16) - Entry level business. Hinges, keyboards, slow performance.
  • ThinkPad P Series (P14s, P15s, P16, P1) - Mobile workstations. GPU issues, overheating, screen problems.

Common ThinkPad Problems We Fix

  • Keyboard Issues - ThinkPad keyboards are excellent but keys do fail over time. The TrackPoint (red nub) can also develop drift issues. We replace keyboards on all ThinkPad models.
  • Screen Problems - The X1 Carbon OLED screens can develop burn-in. We also see dead pixels, backlight issues and cracked screens across the range. Screen replacements available.
  • Battery Degradation - Business laptops get heavy daily use. ThinkPad batteries wear out and need replacing. We source replacement batteries for most models.
  • Docking Station Issues - USB-C and Thunderbolt docking problems are common on ThinkPads. Sometimes its the dock, sometimes its the laptop port.
  • Fan Noise and Overheating - The X1 Carbon runs hot under load. Thermal paste replacement and fan cleaning helps significantly.
  • Hinge Problems - The Yoga convertibles can develop hinge play over time. The 360 degree mechanism wears out.

Lenovo IdeaPad Repairs

The IdeaPad is Lenovos consumer laptop range. We see alot of IdeaPads from home users and students. They are generally good value machines but there are some common issues.

Common Lenovo IdeaPad Models We Repair

  • IdeaPad 3 (14, 15, 17 inch) - Budget range. Hinges, slow performance, charging issues.
  • IdeaPad 5 (14, 15, 16 inch) - Mid-range. Better build but similar issues. Screen problems, keyboards.
  • IdeaPad 5 Pro (14, 16 inch) - Premium. Screen issues on the high resolution displays.
  • IdeaPad Slim 5 (14, 16 inch) - Thin and light. Overheating, fan noise, fragile construction.
  • IdeaPad Gaming 3 (15, 16 inch) - Entry gaming. Thermal issues, fan failures.
  • IdeaPad Flex 5 (14, 16 inch) - Convertible. Hinge wear, touchscreen problems.

Common IdeaPad Problems We Fix

  • Hinge Failures - This is very common on IdeaPads especially the budget IdeaPad 3 range. The plastic hinge mounts crack and the screen becomes loose. We can often repair without replacing the whole chassis.
  • Slow Performance - Many IdeaPads come with slower processors or mechanical hard drives. An SSD upgrade makes a huge difference to speed.
  • Charging Port Issues - The DC jack can work loose or fail. USB-C charging ports on newer models can also develop problems.
  • Keyboard Problems - Keys stop working or become unresponsive. Spill damage is common.
  • Screen Issues - Cracked screens, dead pixels, backlight failures. We stock common IdeaPad screens.
  • Overheating - The slim models run hot. Thermal paste dries out and fans clog with dust.

Lenovo Yoga Repairs

Lenovo Yoga laptops are convertible 2-in-1 machines that can fold back into tablet mode. The 360 degree hinge mechanism is clever but can develop issues over time.

Common Lenovo Yoga Models We Repair

  • Yoga 7 (14, 16 inch) - Mid-range convertible. Hinge wear, touchscreen issues, battery drain.
  • Yoga 9 (14 inch) - Premium convertible. OLED screen issues, speaker problems, overheating.
  • Yoga Slim 7 (13, 14, 15 inch) - Thin and light. Fan noise, thermal throttling.
  • Yoga 6 (13 inch) - Budget convertible. Fabric lid, hinge issues, screen problems.
  • Yoga Pro 9 (16 inch) - Creator laptop. GPU issues, overheating, screen problems.

Common Yoga Problems We Fix

  • Hinge Wear and Failures - The 360 degree hinges take alot of stress. They can become loose or stiff over time. Sometimes just needs adjustment, sometimes full replacement.
  • Touchscreen Issues - Screen stops responding to touch, ghost touches, calibration problems. Sometimes a driver issue, sometimes hardware.
  • OLED Screen Problems - The Yoga 9 OLED can develop burn-in or dead pixels. Screen replacements are expensive but available.
  • Battery Swelling - Older Yoga models can develop battery swelling. This pushes up the keyboard or trackpad. Needs sorting promptly.
  • Pen and Stylus Issues - Active pen stops working or loses connectivity. Sometimes the pen, sometimes the laptop.

Lenovo Legion Gaming Laptop Repairs

Legion is Lenovos gaming laptop brand. Powerful machines with dedicated graphics cards that run hot during gaming. We see alot of Legion laptops from gamers and content creators across the Hills District.

Common Lenovo Legion Models We Repair

  • Legion 5 (15, 16 inch) - Mainstream gaming. Thermal issues, fan failures, keyboard problems.
  • Legion 5 Pro (16 inch) - Higher spec. Same issues but more powerful.
  • Legion 7 (16 inch) - Premium gaming. GPU issues, screen problems, liquid metal thermal paste issues.
  • Legion Pro 5 (16 inch) - Latest generation. Thermal management, fan noise.
  • Legion Pro 7 (16 inch) - Top of the line. Complex cooling system, expensive to repair.
  • Legion Slim 5/7 (14, 16 inch) - Thin gaming laptops. Thermal throttling, overheating.

Common Legion Problems We Fix

  • Overheating and Thermal Throttling - Gaming laptops run hot. The Legion 7 uses liquid metal thermal paste which can leak if not applied correctly. We do full thermal services and repastes.
  • Fan Failures - Fans wear out faster on gaming laptops. Loud noise, grinding sounds or fans not spinning at all.
  • GPU Issues - Graphics card problems, artifacting on screen, crashes during games. Sometimes driver related, sometimes hardware.
  • Keyboard Problems - Gaming keyboards get heavy use. RGB lighting failures, key switches wearing out.
  • Screen Issues - High refresh rate screens can develop problems. Dead pixels, backlight bleed, response time issues.
  • Power and Charging - Gaming laptops need alot of power. Charging port issues, power adapter failures.
