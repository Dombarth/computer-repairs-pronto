Same Day Virus Removal

We can usually offer same day turnaround on virus removal jobs. Most virus infections can be cleaned up in a few hours. For more serious infections like ransomware it may take longer depending on what is required.

Signs Your Computer Has a Virus

Common signs include slow performance, pop-up ads, browser redirects, strange programs running, and messages saying your computer is infected. If your computer is behaving strangely it may have a virus.

Protection After Removal

After removing the infection we can set up proper antivirus protection to help prevent future infections. We can also check for any damage caused by the virus and fix any issues.
