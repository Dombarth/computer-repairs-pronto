Laptop Repairs Hills District

At Computer Repairs Pronto we handle laptop repairs for customers across the Hills District. The team have been repairing laptops for over 20 years and we have seen pretty much every problem there is. Screen replacements, keyboard issues, charging ports, batteries, overheating problems, you name it.

We are based near Castle Hill and service the entire Hills District including Kellyville, Rouse Hill, Bella Vista, Dural and surrounding suburbs.

Common Laptop Problems We Fix

A significant part of our work involves fixing laptops that other places have said cant be repaired. We get called in fairly regularly to fix jobs that other repair shops have made worse or given up on.

  • Cracked or broken laptop screens
  • Keyboard replacements and sticky keys
  • Charging port repairs and DC jack issues
  • Battery replacements
  • Overheating and fan problems
  • Hard drive failures and SSD upgrades
  • Liquid damage and spills
  • Slow performance and software issues
  • Virus and malware removal
  • Motherboard repairs

Same Day Laptop Repairs

For straightforward repairs we can often offer same day turnaround depending on parts availablity. Screen replacements and keyboard repairs can usually be done same day if we have the parts in stock. More complex repairs like motherboard issues or liquid damage may take a few days depending on what is required.

SSD Upgrades

We do alot of hard drive to SSD upgrades for customers wanting to speed up older laptops. If your laptop is running slow this is usually the first thing we recomend looking at. An SSD upgrade can make an old laptop feel like new again.
