Small Business IT Services

We understand that small businesses have different IT needs than large corporations. You need reliable IT that works without breaking the bank. We offer practical solutions that fit small business budgets.

What We Offer

  • Computer Repairs - Fix broken computers, laptops and hardware. Minimise downtime for your business.
  • Network Setup - Set up business networks, wifi, file sharing and printers.
  • Server Maintenance - Keep your server running smoothly. Updates, backups, troubleshooting.
  • Email Setup - Microsoft 365, Google Workspace and business email configuration.
  • Backup Solutions - Protect your business data with proper backup systems.
  • Security - Antivirus, firewalls and security best practices.
  • Software Installation - Install and configure business software.
  • Hardware Procurement - Source computers and equipment for your business.

No Lock-In Contracts

We dont do managed service contracts or monthly retainers for small businesses. You call us when you need us and we charge for the work we do. No ongoing costs when things are working fine. This approach suits most small businesses who dont need a full time IT person but do need someone reliable when issues come up.

Computer Repairs for Business

We repair business laptops and desktops from all major brands including Dell Latitude and Precision, HP EliteBook and ProBook, Lenovo ThinkPad and others.

Common Business Computer Issues

  • Computer wont turn on or wont boot
  • Blue screen errors and crashes
  • Laptop screen damage
  • Keyboard and trackpad issues
  • Hard drive failures and data loss
  • Printer connection problems
  • Software crashes and errors

Business Network Support

A reliable network is essential for modern businesses. We set up and maintain business networks including wired ethernet, wifi, file sharing and network printers. When network issues arise we can troubleshoot and get things working again.

Network Services

  • Network Setup - Design and install networks for small offices.
  • Wifi Configuration - Set up reliable business wifi with proper security.
  • File Sharing - Configure network drives and file sharing between computers.
  • Network Printers - Set up printers so everyone in the office can use them.
  • Network Troubleshooting - Fix connectivity issues and slow network problems.
  • Router and Switch Configuration - Set up and configure network hardware.
