Board Level Repair in Sydney, NSW

At Computer Repairs Pronto we specialise in board level repair and component level diagnostics for customers across Sydney and western Sydney. This is not something that every repair shop offers. Board level work requires micro soldering equipment, proper diagnostics tools and technicians who actually know what they are doing at the component level. The team have been carrying out board level repairs for over fifteen years and we are fully equipped to work on motherboards, logic boards, PCBs and circuit boards across a range of devices.

We carry out all board level repair work on site at our workshop. We do not send boards off to third parties and we do not use subcontractors for any of the soldering or diagnostics work. All repairs come with a warranty on parts and labour.

What Board Level Repair Covers

Board level repair is where the technician works directly on the circuit board itself rather than just swapping out entire modules or components. This includes micro soldering, reflow work, chip replacement, capacitor and resistor replacement and trace repair. The team at Computer Repairs Pronto carry out board level repairs on:

  • Laptop motherboards
  • MacBook and iMac logic boards
  • Gaming console main boards
  • Monitor driver boards and power supply boards
  • PCBs and industrial circuit boards
  • Audio equipment boards

If its a board and its not working properly theres a good chance we can fix it. Some boards are beyond repair but we will always assess first and let you know before carrying out any work.

Logic Board and Motherboard Repairs

Logic board repairs and motherboard repairs are some of the more common board level jobs we handle. For Apple devices this usually involves MacBook logic board faults including no power, no display, liquid damage and charging issues. For Windows laptops and desktops its usually motherboard failures related to power delivery, VRM faults or failed components around the CPU socket area.

We also carry out motherboard repairs on gaming consoles where the main board has failed. This includes HDMI retimer chip replacement on PlayStation and Xbox consoles and general component level fault finding on Nintendo Switch boards. As such we are able to handle repairs that alot of other shops in the area would simply replace the whole board for.

Where We Are

We are based in western Sydney and the majority of our board level repair customers come from Parramatta, Castle Hill, Baulkham Hills, the hills district and surrounding areas. We do get customers driving in from across Sydney and further out which is fine. Board level work is specialised enough that people are willing to travel for it.

Questions

How long does a board level repair take?

Most are same day or next business day. Complex jobs can take longer depending on parts availability.

Is board level repair worth it or should I just replace the board?

Depends on the device and the fault. In many cases board level repair is significantly cheaper than a full board replacement. The team can advise once they have assessed the board.

Do you repair PCBs for commercial equipment?

Yes. We work on commercial and industrial PCBs as well as consumer device boards.
