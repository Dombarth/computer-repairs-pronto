Electronics Repair in Sydney, NSW

At Computer Repairs Pronto we specialise in the areas of electronics repair for residential and commercial customers across Sydney and western Sydney. The team have been carrying out electronics repairs and fault finding from our workshop for over fifteen years and we are fully equipped to handle pretty much anything that comes through the door from consumer electronics through to commercial and industrial equipment.

We do not send your device off to anyone else. All electronics repair work is carried out on site by our technicians which is how we keep turnaround times as fast as they are. We are fully insured and hold public liability and all electronics repair work carried out comes with a warranty on parts and labour. As such we are able to offer a level of service that alot of the other electronics repair shops in the Sydney area simply do not.

What We Fix

The team at Computer Repairs Pronto carry out electronics repairs across a wide range of devices and equipment. Some of the more common jobs we handle include:

  • Consumer electronics and gadgets
  • Audio equipment and hi fi systems
  • Gaming consoles
  • Monitors and display units
  • PCB and board level component repairs
  • Charging port and connector faults
  • Power supply diagnostics
  • General fault finding and diagnostics

Thats the main stuff but we handle alot of jobs that dont fit neatly into a list. If its something outside of the above give us a call and the team can let you know if its something we can help with.

Electronics Diagnostics and Fault Finding

A significant part of our work involves electronics diagnostics and fault finding for devices where the issue is not immediately obvious. The technicians at Computer Repairs Pronto are trained in board level diagnostics including visual inspection, multimeter testing, thermal imaging and component level analysis. We work on single layer and multilayer PCBs and are equiped to handle surface mount and through hole component work.

For commerical and industrial electronics we offer fault finding services on site or at our workshop depending on what is required. We have worked with a range of businesses across Sydney including manufacturing, medical equipment suppliers and retail operations.

Areas We Service

We are based in western Sydney and service customers from across the greater Sydney area. The majority of our electronics repair customers come from the local area including Parramatta, Castle Hill, Baulkham Hills, Northmead and the hills district. We also get customers coming in from further afield across Sydney and the north shore which is no problem at all.

For customers who cant get to us we can discuss options over the phone. Most electronics repairs do require a physical inspection though so in most cases we will need the device brought in to the workshop.
