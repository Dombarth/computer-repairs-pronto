How Workshop Repairs Work

  1. Call First - Give us a call to discuss the problem and arrange a time to drop off.
  2. Drop Off - Bring your computer to our workshop near Castle Hill.
  3. Assessment - We diagnose the problem and provide a quote before starting any work.
  4. Repair - Once you approve the quote we get to work fixing the issue.
  5. Pickup - We call you when its ready for pickup. Most repairs done same day or next day.

Why Choose Workshop Repairs?

  • Same Day Service - For common repairs we can often get your computer back to you same day.
  • Proper Equipment - Our workshop has the tools and equipment needed for all types of repairs.
  • Parts On Hand - We stock common parts like screens, keyboards, batteries and SSDs.
  • Cost Effective - Workshop repairs are generally cheaper than on-site callouts.
  • Quality Work - We can take the time to do the job properly without rushing.

What We Repair at the Workshop

Laptop Repairs

Most laptop repairs are done at the workshop. Common laptop issues we fix include:

  • Cracked or broken screens
  • Keyboard replacements
  • Charging port repairs
  • Battery replacements
  • Hinge repairs
  • Overheating and fan issues
  • Hard drive and SSD replacements
  • Motherboard repairs
  • Liquid damage repairs

We repair all major laptop brands including Dell, HP, Lenovo, Asus, Acer and others.

Desktop PC Repairs

Desktop PC repairs are also done at the workshop. You can bring in your tower or all-in-one PC. Common desktop issues we fix include:

  • Computer wont turn on
  • Blue screen errors and crashes
  • Slow performance
  • Hard drive failures
  • Power supply issues
  • Graphics card problems
  • RAM issues
  • Motherboard repairs

Mac Repairs

We also repair Apple MacBooks and iMacs at the workshop. Common Mac repairs include screen replacements, battery replacements, keyboard issues and data recovery.

Same Day Repairs

For many common repairs we can offer same day turnaround. This includes:

  • Screen replacements (if we have the screen in stock)
  • Keyboard replacements
  • Battery replacements
  • SSD upgrades
  • RAM upgrades
  • Virus removal
  • Windows reinstallation

More complex repairs like motherboard issues, liquid damage, or parts we need to order may take a few days.

Turnaround Times

  • Same Day - Common repairs with parts in stock
  • Next Day - Most standard repairs
  • 2-3 Days - Repairs needing parts ordered
  • 1 Week+ - Complex motherboard repairs or difficult parts to source

We always provide an estimate of turnaround time when you drop off your computer.

Free Assessment and Quote

We provide a free assessment and quote before starting any repair work. There is no charge if you decide not to go ahead with the repair. This way you know exactly what the problem is and what it will cost to fix before committing.

Parts and Warranty

We use quality replacement parts that meet or exceed OEM specifications. All repairs come with a warranty on parts and labour. If something goes wrong with the repair we fix it.
