Monitor Repair in Sydney, NSW

At Computer Repairs Pronto we specialise in monitor repairs for customers across Sydney and western Sydney. The team carry out repairs on LCD monitors, LED monitors, OLED monitors, gaming monitors and commercial display units. We have been repairing monitors for over fifteen years and we are equiped to handle everything from basic power supply faults through to board level repairs on driver boards and main boards.

All monitor repair work is carried out on site at our workshop. We do not send monitors off to third parties. We are fully insured and all monitor repair work comes with a warranty on parts and labour. As such we are able to offer same day diagnostics on most monitors that come through the door.

Monitor Faults We Repair

The most common monitor faults we see are no power, no display, flickering, backlight failure and HDMI port issues. The technicians at Computer Repairs Pronto work at the board level which means we can repair the actual fault rather than just replacing entire boards. This often makes repair more cost effective than what other shops would quote for the same job.

Some of the specific monitor repairs we carry out include:

  • No power and dead monitor faults
  • No display and blank screen issues
  • HDMI port replacement
  • Power supply board repair
  • Main board and driver board repair
  • Backlight and inverter faults
  • Colour issues and display artifacts

We work on standard monitors, ultrawide monitors, curved monitors and gaming monitors. Panel types include LCD, LED, IPS and OLED. If the panel itself is cracked or has dead pixels that is generally not repairable but most other monitor faults can be fixed at the board level.

LCD and LED Monitor Repair

LCD and LED monitors are the most common type we repair. Faults are usually on the power supply board or the main driver board. Capacitor failure is extremely common on older LCD monitors and is a straightforward repair in most cases. For LED monitors we also see backlight driver faults and LED strip failures which we can repair or replace depending on the model.

OLED Monitor Repair

OLED monitor repairs are less common but we are seeing more of them as OLED monitors become more widely used for gaming and creative work. Common faults include board level issues, connector faults and in some cases burn in related problems. We assess each OLED monitor on a case by case basis and provide an honest assessment of whether repair is viable.

Monitor Repair Across Sydney

We are based in western Sydney and service customers from Parramatta, Castle Hill, Baulkham Hills, the hills district and surrounding areas. Monitors can be dropped off at our workshop. For larger or heavier commercial display units we can discuss logistics. We also get customers from across Sydney and the north shore driving in for monitor repairs.
