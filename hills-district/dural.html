The team have been servicing Dural since 2006. Its one of those semi rural suburbs where alot of people work from home or run businesses from their properties. Alot of our Dural customers have been coming to us for years now.

Why Dural Customers Come to Us

Dural has a mix of acreage properties and more suburban areas. Alot of people there have home offices or small businesses that rely on their computers. The team at Computer Repairs Pronto have helped plenty of Dural customers over the years with everything from general repairs to setting up proper backup systems. As such we understand that downtime costs money and we try to get machines back as quick as we can.

How We Work

When you bring a machine in we have a look at it first and work out whats going on. We give you a quote before doing any work. If its something simple we can often get it done same day. More complex jobs might take a few days depending on whats needed. We keep you updated if anything changes.

All repairs come with a warranty on parts and labour. We use quality parts becuase cheap parts cause more problems down the track.

Workshop or Onsite

Workshop Repairs

Onsite Visits
